Bot creation notes:

Twitter: Throw in a username - the stuff after the @. This will retrieve the last 3200 tweets, not including retweets and replies. The more tweets, the better - more text means more chances to generate interesting things.

NLTK: Select an id from the list here. These are generally intended as 'sample' texts and may not yield the most...comprehensible bots. They're really trying their best though!

Text files: Works best with files that contain big strings of text, split up by common English punctuation. You can upload one or many - there's a lot of potential here, both for fun and failure! .txt files only, please~

Bots are unpredictable and can be finicky. If your bot isn't really feeling up to it, it may not say much. If it never says anything, feel free to mail me and I'll see what I can do.

Finally...all information that bots spit out is entirely public, including the username of whoever created it. By going through this process and clicking that (wonderfully enticing) 'create' button, you accept this fact and any consequences that may arise. Please treat your bots with care!
